Question 1: Explain the role of machine learning in modern computing.

Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that focuses on building systems
that learn from data. Instead of being explicitly programmed to perform a task, these
systems use algorithms to parse data, learn from it, and then make predictions or
decisions. The field has grown tremendously in recent years, driven by increases in
computational power and the availability of large datasets. Deep learning, a further
subset of machine learning, uses neural networks with many layers to model complex
patterns in data. Applications range from image recognition and natural language
processing to autonomous vehicles and medical diagnosis.
Supervised learning, unsupervised learning, and reinforcement learning represent the
three main paradigms in the field today. Model evaluation often relies on held out
validation data, and optimization methods like gradient descent are used to tune
parameters by minimizing a loss function.
