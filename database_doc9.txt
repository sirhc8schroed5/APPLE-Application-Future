Question 9: Explain observability in production systems.

Observability relies on metrics, logs, and traces to understand system behavior.
Metrics provide time series signals used to track service level objectives. Logs capture
discrete events with context, while distributed tracing links requests across services.
Dashboards and alerts help teams detect regressions quickly and diagnose incidents. A
well instrumented system makes failures visible and debuggable.
