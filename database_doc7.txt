Question 7: Explain optimization techniques in machine learning.

Optimization in machine learning often relies on gradient descent to minimize a loss
function. A learning rate controls step size; too large can diverge, too small slows
convergence. Regularization reduces overfitting by penalizing large weights. Stochastic
gradient descent uses mini-batches for efficiency on large datasets. Convex objectives
have global minima, while nonconvex objectives require careful tuning and monitoring.
